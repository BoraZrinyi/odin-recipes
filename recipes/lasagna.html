Lasagna

Description

What are the correct layers for lasagna? Start with a layer of lasagna noodles in the bottom of the baking dish, then layer in the cottage cheese mixture, sliced mozzarella, and finally, the meat sauce. Repeat these layers once, then sprinkle parmesan right on top. Is it difficult? Well...

I can make it much more simple.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tesco lasagna sheet
  • Tesco Red Sauce
  • Tesco White Sauce
  • Some Cheddar chease

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a little oil.
  2. Add the red sauce in the jar and cook together for 15 minutes.
  3. Place a layer of lasagna sheets in the bottom of the baking dish.
  4. A layer of red sauce-meat comes on top.
  5. A layer of white sauce comes on top.
  6. A layer of lasagna sheets comes on top.
  7. A layer of red sauce comes on top.
  8. A layer of white sauce comes on top.
  9. Sprinkle with the grated cheddar.
  10. Put in the oven and bake for 20-25 minutes.

    11. And that's it.
